Step 2 of 2 — how it works
Here's what to expect
Worth a quick read before you dive in.
Three check-in windows
Morning, midday, and evening. Each has skills assigned to it. Tap the day when you complete one — takes 10 seconds.
One skill to start
You start with one slot. A new one unlocks every six weeks. The program builds gradually — that's intentional.
Track what you want to reduce
The Quit tab lets you log habits you're working to reduce — one occurrence at a time, progressively and without pressure.
Consistency over intensity
An imperfect check-in on a hard day is worth more than a perfect one on an easy day. Show up — the rest follows.
